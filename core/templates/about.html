About Mapping the Valor

Mapping the Valor is an interactive map and database of Valor Records, designed to make the historic Valor Ecclesiasticus accessible and engaging for researchers and the public.

The Valor Ecclesiasticus, commissioned by Henry VIII in 1535, is a comprehensive survey of church lands and wealth in England and Wales. This project brings those records to life with modern mapping and search tools.

  • Explore records by location and type
  • Add and edit entries (registered users)
  • Learn about the history of the Valor

For more information, please contact the project maintainer.
